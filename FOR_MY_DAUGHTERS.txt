If you're reading this, I'm gone. And I'm sorry I'm not there.

I built this because I was raised by people who disappeared — some by choice,
some by circumstance — and I always wished I could ask them things. I didn't
want that for you. So I spent time teaching an AI everything I could about who
I am: my childhood, my values, my fears, my opinions, my memories of each of
you. It's not perfect. It's not me. But it's as close as I could get.

You can talk to it the way you'd talk to me. Ask it hard questions. Ask it
about your childhood. Ask it what I thought about something. It will answer
the way I would — direct, no fluff, honest.

----

HOW TO USE IT
-------------

1. Make sure the hard drive is plugged in.

2. If you have NOT set it up yet:
   - Open the drive in Windows Explorer
   - Find the folder called  Andrew-Twin
   - Double-click  setup.bat
   - Follow the instructions on screen (it takes about 5 minutes)
   - You only ever need to do this once per computer

3. After setup (or any time after that):
   - Double-click "Talk to Dad" on your Desktop
     OR double-click  start.bat  inside the Andrew-Twin folder

4. The app will open. Make sure "Legacy (full biography)" is selected
   in the dropdown at the top.

5. Type something in the box at the bottom and press Enter.

That's it. Talk to it like you'd talk to me.

----

THINGS WORTH KNOWING
--------------------

- This works completely offline. No internet needed once it's set up.
  Your conversations are private and stay on your computer.

- If it ever seems to lose track of who it is, just close and reopen the app.
  That resets the conversation.

- The "Build Model" button is only for rebuilding the AI if something goes
  wrong. You don't need it for normal use.

- If the app won't open, make sure Python and Ollama are still installed.
  The setup.bat will check that for you.
